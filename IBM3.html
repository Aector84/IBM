About IBM

International Business Machines Corporation (IBM) is a multinational technology and consulting company. Founded in 1911, IBM has played a significant role in the development of computers and technology throughout the years.

History

IBM's history is marked by groundbreaking innovations, from the development of the IBM 360 mainframe in the 1960s to the introduction of the personal computer in the 1980s. Today, IBM continues to be a leader in cloud computing, artificial intelligence, and more.
